# California Termination & Offboarding (2026)

## 1. Final Pay
- [ ] **Fired**: Pay Immediately.
- [ ] **Resign (72+ hrs)**: Pay on last day.
- [ ] **Resign (<72 hrs)**: Pay within 72 hours.

## 2. Mandatory Paperwork
- [ ] Change in Relationship Notice.
- [ ] EDD Brochure (DE 2320).
- [ ] HIPP Notice / COBRA.
